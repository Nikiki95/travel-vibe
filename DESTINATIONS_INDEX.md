## 💡 Curation Philosophy

✅ **Inclusion Criteria:**
- Authentic, non-touristy experiences
- Unique, one-of-a-kind locations
- Accessible (difficult but doable)
- Emotionally resonant
- Visually compelling
- Educational/transformative

❌ **Exclusion Criteria:**
- Generic resort destinations
- Overly touristy (Disneyland, etc.)
- No good free photos available
- Impossible to reach safely
- Similar to existing destination
- Lacking unique cultural value
